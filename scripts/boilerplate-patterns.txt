# Boilerplate line patterns for Clean-HtmlArtifacts
# Each line is a regex matched with (?im) flags against the full markdown text.
# Lines starting with # are comments and are ignored.

^\s*Subscribe to (our )?newsletter.*$
^\s*Follow us on.*$
^\s*Cookie (policy|settings|preferences).*$
^\s*Accept (all )?cookies.*$
^\s*Privacy Policy.*$
^\s*Terms of (Service|Use).*$
^\s*All rights reserved\.?\s*$
^\s*Loading\.{0,3}\s*$
^\s*Advertisement\s*$
^\s*Skip to (main )?content.*$
^\s*Back to top\s*$
